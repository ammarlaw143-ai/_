⛏️ Minecraft Complete Guide

Commands, enchantments, redstone tutorials, survival tips & creative building.

Essential Commands

Enable cheats in your world settings, then open chat with T and type these commands.

CommandDescription
/gamemode creativeSwitch to Creative mode
/gamemode survivalSwitch to Survival mode
/time set daySet time to day
/weather clearClear weather
/give @p diamond 64Give yourself 64 diamonds
/tp @p ~ ~10 ~Teleport 10 blocks up
/effect give @p speed 999 2Speed II for 999 seconds
/locate structure strongholdFind nearest stronghold
/fill ~-5 ~-1 ~-5 ~5 ~-1 ~5 diamond_blockReplace floor with diamonds
/summon iron_golemSpawn an iron golem
/kill @e[type=!player]Remove all entities except players
/setworldspawnSet current location as world spawn

Survival Tutorial — First Night

  1. Punch trees — Collect at least 16 oak logs immediately
  2. Craft tools — Make a crafting table, wooden pickaxe, axe, and sword
  3. Find food — Kill animals or harvest wheat from villages
  4. Mine stone — Upgrade to stone tools before sunset
  5. Build shelter — Dig into a hillside or build a 5×5 box with a door
  6. Light it up — Craft torches from coal/charcoal to prevent mob spawns
  7. Find iron — Mine at Y-level 16 for best iron ore rates

Best Enchantments

  • Sword: Sharpness V, Looting III, Unbreaking III, Mending
  • Pickaxe: Efficiency V, Fortune III, Unbreaking III, Mending
  • Armor: Protection IV on all pieces, Thorns III optional
  • Bow: Power V, Infinity, Unbreaking III, Flame

Redstone Basics

Simple Automatic Door

Place a pressure plate in front of a door connected with redstone dust to a piston door mechanism. When you step on the plate, the pistons retract and the door opens automatically.

Auto Farm

Use water streams, hoppers, and chests to collect crop drops automatically. Plant seeds with a dispenser and use observers to detect when crops are fully grown.

Cayo Perico Heist — Solo Elite

  1. Scout the island using the reconnaissance mission
  2. Choose Drainage Pipe as entry point and Main Dock as escape
  3. Take out the guard with the bolt cutters first
  4. Use the grappling hook to enter the compound silently
  5. Grab primary target + secondary loot in the vault
  6. Escape via the main dock — sub or fast boat
  7. Elite Challenge: Complete in under 14 minutes, no damage, all loot

🎯 Call of Duty Pro Guide

Meta loadouts, ranked strategies, movement tech, and map control for Black Ops 6 & Warzone.

Meta Multiplayer Loadout — Assault

SlotAttachment
PrimaryXM4 — Compensator, Long Barrel, 40 Round Mag, Reflex Sight, Stabilizer
Secondary9mm PM or combat knife for speed
Perk 1Flak Jacket (explosive resistance)
Perk 2Tactical Mask (counter flash/stun)
Perk 3G.O.A.T. Gloves (faster weapon swap)
LethalFrag Grenade
TacticalStun Grenade or Smoke
WildcardOverkill (second primary weapon)

Movement Tech

  • Slide Cancel: Sprint → Slide → Jump → ADS to reset sprint instantly
  • Dolphin Dive: Sprint + dive over cover to surprise enemies below
  • Drop Shot: Go prone mid-gunfight while maintaining aim
  • Jump Peek: Quick jump around corners to gather info without full exposure
  • Strafe Shooting: Move left-right while firing to make yourself harder to hit

Ranked Play Tips

  1. Always use a headset — sound cues win gunfights
  2. Play the objective in Hardpoint and Control
  3. Pre-aim common angles when rotating between bomb sites
  4. Communicate enemy positions to your team immediately
  5. Learn one map thoroughly before expanding
